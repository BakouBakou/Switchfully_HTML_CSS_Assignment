Who am I?

Hello! My name is Baker. I'm 29 years old and live in Namur.

I've studied bioengineering at the Catholic University of Louvain-la-Neuve and graduated in 2016. I already had an interest in IT at that time and tried to pick my jobs in consequence. That lead me to work 2 years in testing as a consultant and then 2 years as a business analyst before realizing that it wasn't what I wanted to do. After a little bit of introspection, I dived into the world of programming and quickly realized that this is the professional life I wanted. This website is but one small step towards that goal.

When I'm not working or spending time with friends/family, you can usually find me playing some video games or working out, and this second point is what I chose to cover as my topic of interest.
